   ==========================================================================
   HANDHELD SIMULATION LOGIC:

   1. MOTION & LAG (MOTION_OFS / RESPONSE_LAG):
      - MOTION_OFS controls the distance of the ghosting trail.
      - RESPONSE_LAG simulates the electrical jitter/delay of liquid crystals.
      - Use high values for DMG/Game Gear to recreate that classic motion blur.

   2. SATURATION: 
      - Non-backlit screens (GBA/GBC) require high saturation (1.3 - 1.4) to 
        match the "Vivid" art style developers used to offset dim displays.
   
   3. GHOSTING (GHOST_STR): 
      - Blends the current frame with previous samples. Essential for STN panels.

   4. COLOR PROFILES:
      - 1 (mGBA): Corrects GBA colors for modern displays.
      - 5 (GBG): Applies the classic "Pea Soup" Green matrix.

   5. EDGE SOFTENING:
      - Since handhelds lack scanlines, use EDGE_SOFT (0.3) to blend pixels 
        naturally, simulating the physical gaps in an LCD grid.
